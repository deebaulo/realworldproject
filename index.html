About Us

Deebley specializes in web design, HTML, CSS coding and Wordpress development. We help small businesses define their web strategies, design the user experience, develop and maintain CMS wordpress websites.

Successful small business owners are driven by their passion to create something meaniningful. They are builders, makers, creators and innovators.

Small businesses are important to our lives and our communities. But building a business is hard and you can't always do it all. So, Deebley is here to lend a hand.

Dee Baulo is the founder of Deebley, an aspiring Web developer based in the Philippines.. Aside from it, she is also an entrepreneur by passion.

You can reach her through her LinkedIn account, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and Youtube Channel.
